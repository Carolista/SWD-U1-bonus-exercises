
SWD UNIT 1 - HTML

It's important to plan out your page before laying down code. 
    - What kind of content, images, links, etc. will you need?
    - In what order do you want to display the content?

Here's an example:

HEADER    
    - Title of blog: The Green thumb
    - Author: Ivana Grostuv

ARTICLE
    SECTION 1:
    Report on today's activities

    SECTION 2: 
    Detail plans for tomorrow:
        - Tomorrow's tasks - use ordered list
        - Each plant's requirements (quantity, cluster?, spacing, depth) - use table

    SECTION 3:
    Share recommendation for bulbs: Breck's https://brecks.com

FOOTER
    - Copyright statement

IMAGES
    - Float general image to right at top (use local file)
    - Use images in info table for flowers - sources:
