Welcome to Main Street Pet Rescue: Where Pets Come First

At Main Street Pet Rescue, pets are at the heart of everything we do. From playful kittens to laidback seniors, we are dedicated to meeting their unique needs—whether it's behavioral care, a routine checkup or addressing complex medical issues.

Our shelter isn't fancy, but it is built with pets in mind, offering cozy beds, soft blankets, fun toys, nutritious food, and — most importantly — the affection of people who adore them.

But what truly sets us apart is how deeply we get to know each pet. We take the time to learn their quirks, preferences, and personalities, ensuring we make meaningful matches between our pets and their new families.

Come visit Main Street Pet Rescue today and see the difference a pet-first shelter makes.
